
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Version history and control, allowing for collaboration, documentation

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Almost any field: Info Sci, Economics, Finance, the Health industry, etc

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merge retains the branches that were merged, while rebasing gets rid of some of them, moving the starting point and then redoing the changes from there
